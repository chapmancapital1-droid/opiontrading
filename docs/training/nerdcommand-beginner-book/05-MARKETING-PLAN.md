# Marketing Plan  
## NerdCommand Beginner Options Desk + OptionScope tools

**Product stack:** Curriculum (this book) + OptionScope software + optional live cohort  
**Brand:** NerdCommand · See · Trust · Own  
**North star metric:** Activated operators (journaled paper trades), **not** follower vanity  

---

## 1. Positioning

### One-liner
**“The beginner options desk that teaches defined-risk process with OptionScope — not signals.”**

### Category
EdTech + personal trading companion (not prop firm, not signal room, not broker).

### Differentiation

| Us | Them |
|----|------|
| Process + software map | Hot tips |
| Seed capital honesty | Fake $25k identity |
| Checklist → you execute | “Auto bot” fantasy |
| Journal calibration | Highlight reels |

---

## 2. Audience segments

| Segment | Pain | Offer |
|---------|------|--------|
| **A. True beginner** | Overwhelmed by YouTube | Book + 4-week cohort |
| **B. App user** | Has OptionScope, underuses tabs | In-app Education + video series |
| **C. Creator/coach** | Needs curriculum to teach | Teacher manual + screenshot pack license (your terms) |
| **D. Seed capital trader** | Blew small account once | Empire ladder + size 0 doctrine |

**Not targeting:** people seeking guaranteed income; under-18; anyone wanting password-based autotrade.

---

## 3. Offer ladder

| Tier | Contents | Price posture |
|------|----------|---------------|
| **Free** | EP0–2 videos · Education tab · install.txt | Attention |
| **Core book** | Full beginner book PDF + 4 strategies | Low $ or lead magnet |
| **Desk course** | Videos 0–11 + worksheets + paper challenge | Mid |
| **Cohort** | 4-week live with teacher manual delivery | Premium |
| **Software** | OptionScope personal use (your distribution model) | Separate / bundle |

**Never bundle:** “we place trades for you.”

---

## 4. Funnel

```
Content (YT/TikTok/Shorts: laws, desk map, size 0 honesty)
    → Free EP0 + lead magnet checklist (07 one-pager)
        → Email: 5-day desk challenge (paper only)
            → Core book / course
                → Cohort
                    → Long-term: empire community (rules-bound)
```

### Lead magnet title options
- “Green Lights Before Any Options Fill (OptionScope)”  
- “4 Safer Beginner Structures — Max Loss Formulas”  
- “Seed Capital Reality Check ($500 Ladder)”

## 5. Channel plan (90 days)

| Channel | Cadence | Content |
|---------|---------|---------|
| YouTube | 1 long / week | Full episodes from 02 |
| Shorts/Reels | 4 / week | 15s: law, size 0, checklist, DEMO badge |
| X / LinkedIn | 5 / week | Operator quotes + UI stills from 03 |
| Email | 2 / week | Challenge + case studies (paper) |
| In-app | Always on | `/education` + Evolve hive story |

### Hook formulas (compliant)
- “The button OptionScope will never have: Buy.”  
- “When size is 0, the software is protecting the empire.”  
- “Delta is not probability of profit — here’s the desk that respects that.”  

### Banned hooks
- “I made $10k in a day — you can too”  
- “Set it and forget it passive options”  
- “AI guarantees 80% wins”

## 7. Trust & compliance

Every public piece includes:

> Educational only. Options risk loss. Not investment advice. Not affiliated with Robinhood. OptionScope does not execute trades. Past or model performance is not future results.

**Testimonials:** only process / clarity — not ROI claims without audited disclaimers.  
**Refunds:** education goods per your policy; never imply trading profits refund.
