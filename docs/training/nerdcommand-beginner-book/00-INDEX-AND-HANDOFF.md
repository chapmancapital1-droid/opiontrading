## 2. Four higher-safety strategies (locked for this curriculum)

Chosen from OptionScope brain + empire seed policy (**defined risk preferred**, CSP blocked at seed):

| Rank | Strategy | Why “safer” for beginners | Seed OK? |
|------|----------|---------------------------|----------|
| 1 | **Bull put credit spread** | Defined max loss; sell premium with support bias | Yes (if 1-lot fits risk ceiling) |
| 2 | **Bear call credit spread** | Defined max loss; resistance / bearish-neutral | Yes |
| 3 | **Iron condor** | Defined range income; forces patience | Paper first; live when equity/width fit |
| 4 | **Bull call debit spread** | Defined risk bullish (no naked long option theater) | Yes — preferred micro directional |

**Explicitly NOT in beginner core:** naked shorts, 0DTE lottery, CSP on seed cash, undefined-risk structures.
