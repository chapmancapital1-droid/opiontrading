HOW TO USE THIS PACK
--------------------
1. Record 10–12 short videos (3–8 min each) OR one long masterclass (45–60 min).
2. Each MODULE has:
   A) AVATAR PROMPT — paste into image/avatar tool for talking-head stills or Grok/avatar video
   B) ON-SCREEN ACTIONS — exact clicks for screen share
   C) SCRIPT — speak this (customize with “I / we” as expert)
   D) B-ROLL / GRAPHICS cues
3. Always end modules with risk disclaimer (see LEGAL TAG at bottom).
4. Brand: dark empire UI, “See · Trust · Own”, no buy/sell buttons that look like broker orders.
