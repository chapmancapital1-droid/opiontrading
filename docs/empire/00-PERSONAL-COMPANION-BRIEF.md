# OptionScope — Personal Empire Companion Brief

**Classification:** Michael Chapman personal use only until real profits proven  
**Company:** NerdCommand / Chapman Capital empire  
**Doctrine:** Long live the empire — capital first, product second for public release  
**Date:** 2026-07-12

## Mission
OptionScope is **not** a public SaaS launch toy yet. It is Michael’s **personal options trading assistant and companion brain** — the system that must help grow real (and paper-rehearsed) capital so the company stays alive.

## Capital ladder (account growth path)
| Phase | Equity target | Intent |
|-------|---------------|--------|
| Seed | **$500** | Survive, learn process, avoid blow-up |
| Stage 1 | **$500 → $5,000** (10×) | Proof of process under small-account constraints |
| Stage 2 | **$5,000 → $25,000** (5×) | Scale defined-risk income + selective growth |

## Companion requirements
1. **Always-on options obsession** — chains, marks, Greeks, structures, real numbers  
2. **Robinhood account data in** — see what was already done; flag what to fix (manual export/CSV or paste first; never steal RH password; no unofficial reverse-engineered RH login)  
3. **Brain assistance** — rank, size, explain, checklist; human executes  
4. **Education for Michael** — personal guide + graphic prompts for every dashboard/feature  
5. **Public release later** — only after real-life profits validate the empire path  

## Hard laws
- Educational companion — not personalized investment advice to third parties  
- No auto-trade  
- Model PoP/EV are estimates  
- Small accounts cannot safely sell naked undefined risk as default  
- $500 options reality: many strategies need more capital than $500 (CSP on $200 stock = $20k cash); design for **defined-risk micro** and **paper until process works**
